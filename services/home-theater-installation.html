Dolby Atmos & Immersive Audio

Surround sound with overhead height channels creates a dome of sound. Systems range from 7.1.4 to full 9.1.6 reference designs with object-based audio that places sound precisely in three-dimensional space.

4K & 8K Projection

Ultra-short-throw laser projectors from Sony, JVC, and Epson deliver massive screens with perfect black levels and HDR. Acoustically transparent screens up to 180 inches allow speakers behind the screen.

Acoustic Design & Treatment

Calibrated absorption, diffusion, and bass management eliminate standing waves and frequency anomalies. Audio sounds exactly as the director intended.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What size room do I need?
A dedicated theater ideally starts at 15'x20' for two rows, but we've designed excellent single-row theaters in spaces as small as 12'x15'.
Q: Media room vs. dedicated theater?
A media room is multipurpose with great AV. A dedicated theater is purpose-built with light control, acoustic treatment, and reference-grade equipment.
Q: Can I stream content?
Yes. We integrate all streaming services plus media servers like Kaleidescape for stunning 4K HDR with lossless audio.
