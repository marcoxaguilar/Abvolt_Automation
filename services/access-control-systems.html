Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I give my housekeeper a temporary code?
Yes. Create time-limited codes that work only during scheduled hours. The system logs entry and can disarm specific zones.
Q: Do smart locks work during power outages?
Battery backup lasts 6-12 months with physical key override always available.
Q: Does access control integrate with cameras?
When someone uses a code or rings the intercom, nearby cameras automatically record, creating a complete visual log.
