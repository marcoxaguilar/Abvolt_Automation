Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much can lighting control save on energy?
Lutron systems typically reduce lighting energy by 20-30% through occupancy sensing, daylight harvesting, and scheduled dimming.
Q: Can you retrofit lighting control into an existing home?
Yes. Lutron RadioRA 3 uses wireless technology that installs into existing switch boxes without new wiring.
Q: What are circadian rhythm lighting systems?
Lighting that automatically adjusts color temperature throughout the day — cooler in morning, warmer in evening — supporting your sleep-wake cycle.
