ACCESS CONTROL FOR LUXURY PROPERTIES: BEYOND SMART LOCKS

December 28, 2025By Abvolt Automation8 min read

Security begins at the perimeter. Comprehensive access control encompasses video verification, automated gates, biometrics, visitor management, and deep automation integration.

Video Intercom: See Before You Open

HD video and two-way audio at every entry. See visitors on your phone, touchscreen, or any TV. Speak, unlock remotely, or ignore — from anywhere in the world.

Managing Household Staff Access

Create individual profiles with specific permissions — certain doors, times, and days. Every entry logged with automation responses like zone-specific disarming.

Integration with Home Automation

When you unlock the front door: lights activate, alarm disarms, playlist starts, thermostat adjusts. When the last person leaves: system arms, lights off, temperature adjusts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are smart locks safe enough?
Modern smart locks use AES encryption and meet commercial standards. Combined with video verification, they exceed traditional lock security.
Q: What if power goes out?
Battery backup lasts 6-12 months. Physical key override always available.
Q: Can I see who entered my home?
Every event logged with timestamp, ID, and associated camera footage.
