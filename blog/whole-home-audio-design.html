WHOLE-HOME AUDIO: DESIGNING THE PERFECT MULTI-ROOM SOUND SYSTEM

January 12, 2026By Abvolt Automation8 min read

Imagine your playlist following you room to room, jazz in the master suite while kids enjoy music downstairs, and crystal-clear outdoor sound — all invisible and effortless.

Understanding Multi-Zone Audio

Independent zones play different sources simultaneously or group together. Typical zones: kitchen, master suite, office, bedrooms, bathrooms, patio, pool.

Speaker Technology: Invisible is Beautiful

In-ceiling and in-wall speakers deliver exceptional performance while completely hidden. Some can be mudded and painted over, disappearing entirely.

Outdoor Audio Done Right

Landscape speakers look like rocks or planters. Subterranean subwoofers deliver deep bass invisibly. Weather-resistant for Chicago's extreme seasons.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Built-in vs Bluetooth speakers?
Built-in provides consistent, high-quality sound throughout your home without batteries or moving speakers.
Q: Can I use vinyl or CDs?
Yes. We integrate turntables and disc players as sources distributed to any zone.
Q: Best system for outdoors?
Coastal Source and Sonance landscape speakers — specifically engineered for outdoor use and extreme weather.
